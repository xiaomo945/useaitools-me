## 🏷️ Content Guidelines

- **Tone**: Professional, warm, direct (like a trusted friend recommending tools)
- **Internal Links**: Always link to relevant tool pages and categories using `[[link:/path|text]]` format
- **Headings**: Use H2 for section titles: `## Section Name`, H1 for main title only
- **Affiliate Links**: Include affiliate links where appropriate
- **SEO**: Optimized descriptions (<= 160 chars), titles that follow formula: Number + Pain Point + Solution
- **Brand Consistency**: Maintain same voice and formatting across all posts
