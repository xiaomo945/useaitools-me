## Quick Reference & Next Steps

### For Tools with Known Affiliate Programs:
1. Visit their affiliate page directly
2. Sign up for the program
3. Add affiliate links to tools.json once approved

### For Tools Without Obvious Affiliate Program:
1. Send the outreach email above
2. Follow up after 5-7 days if no response
3. Look for alternative contact methods on their website

### Tracking Links:
Once approved, add affiliate links to:
- `data/tools.json` → `affiliate_link` field
- Update blog posts with new affiliate links
- Update any internal links to use affiliate codes
