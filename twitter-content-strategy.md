## Posting Schedule

### Frequency
- **Target**: 3-5 tweets per week
- **Minimum**: 2 tweets per week
- **Maximum**: 1 tweet per day

### Best Times (EST)
- **Monday**: 9:00-10:00 AM
- **Wednesday**: 9:00-10:00 AM
- **Friday**: 9:00-10:00 AM

### Content Calendar Template

**Week of {date}:**
- **Monday**: Build in Public (progress update)
- **Wednesday**: Tool Recommendation (specific tool)
- **Friday**: Engagement (question/poll) OR Indie Maker Insight

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily (15 minutes)
1. Reply to 5-10 tweets from #buildinpublic community
2. Like 10-15 relevant tweets
3. Retweet 2-3 valuable insights

### Weekly (30 minutes)
1. Follow 10-20 new accounts in AI/indie maker space
2. Engage with 5 larger accounts (1k-10k followers)
3. Share 1-2 threads from others (with commentary)

### Response Guidelines
- **Respond within 24 hours** to all mentions and DMs
- **Be genuine**: Thank people, answer questions, share more
- **Never**: Argue, defend, or get defensive
- **Always**: Add value, be helpful, stay positive

## Metrics to Track

### Weekly
- Follower growth
- Tweet impressions
- Engagement rate (likes + replies + retweets / impressions)
- Link clicks

### Monthly
- Total follower count
- Average engagement rate
- Top performing tweets
- Traffic from Twitter to website
